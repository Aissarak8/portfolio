# Why I'm Learning in Public

Hi, I'm **Aissa** — an Applied Computer Science student in Marrakech, and this is the first post on my blog.

I started this blog for a simple reason: **the best way to learn is to build in public.** Instead of studying quietly and hoping it sticks, I want to document what I learn, share the projects I build, and be honest about where I am in the journey.

## What "learning in public" means to me

- **Building real projects**, not just following tutorials.
- **Writing down what I learn** so it actually sticks (and maybe helps someone else).
- **Being honest** about my level — I'm a beginner in a lot of things, and that's fine.

## Where I am right now

I'm most comfortable with **HTML, CSS**, and the basics of **Python, Java, and C**. I'm actively learning **JavaScript, React, and Node.js**, and I'm exploring **AI agents and automation with n8n**.

## What's next

My goal is to become an **AI Engineer**. Over the next year I'll be building projects across web, AI, and automation — and writing about each one here.

If you're on a similar path, thanks for reading. Let's build. 🚀
